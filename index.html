Welcome

The interdisciplinary computational intelligence seminar is a weekly seminar in the School of Information Sciences and Arts. Each week we will discuss papers and share our original research in areas including computer vision, visualization, machine learning, AI, and natural language processing.

Time: Fridays, 1:30pm - 3:00pm
Location: GS 906 (Unless otherwise noted below)
